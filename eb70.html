YOUR BEST TOOL - MICA 5.5mm Diameter Wireless Endoscope:

 

The MICA EB70 Endoscope is the newest upgrade version developed based on the all Endoscopes in the market, which is the smallest camera size that can be achieved by current technology without sacrifice the camera’s performance. Featured with the 2.0MP 1080P high-resolution camera chip, the MICA WiFi & Wireless inspection camera can easy to extend your eyes and approach to the places hard to reach, hence it can help to locate and solve potential problems easily and quickly, saving your time and money.

 

A new world is waiting for you to explore

Seeing more clearer and further

 

Featured with 2.0 MP camera and flexible semi-rigid cable, The MICA wireless endoscope EB70 help you easy to capture clear picture and take fluent video in dark area or multiple conditions of what you’re studying, analyzing or measuring. Besides, it works perfectly on android and ios smartphone and tablets, making it widely used for home inspections, notice electrical wire, dredge sewer drain pipe, HVAC, auto repair and industrial applications.

 

Product information

 

* 2.0 Megapixels CMOS Camera Chip

* Camera Resolution: 1920x1080, 1280x720, 640x480

* 5.5mm Diameter Smallest Camera Probe

* 6500K Daylight LED Technology

* 6pcs Adjustable Brightness LED Light

* IP68 Waterproof (for camera len/tube only）

* 2600mAh Li-lon Rechargeable Battery

* Image and video stored in JPG and AVI format

 

0.216 inch Diameter Camera

 

MICA EB70 Endoscope come with as a 5.5mm (0.216 inch) diameter camera ensure you can easily access to narrower places and perfect examining difficult-to-reach areas such as under car spark plugs, plumbing pipe, wall drilling, cylinder and more.

 

IP68 Waterproof

 

This camera head meets with IP68 waterproof standards which allows working in wet, moist or underwater inspection working conditions (no deeper than 3.3ft) such as sewer and pipeline inspection. In addition, for better performance, please keep the lens 1.18-3.94 inch in front of the object.

 

PLEASE NOTE:

 

* The default resolution of recording video is 640 x 480.

* Need to be connected with your phone via WiFi on your phone settings, instead of the app.

* The focal distance is flexible to get clear image, the best observation distance between the camera and the object is 1.18-3.94 inches.
