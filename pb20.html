Enhanced Bass and Wooden Accent

 

The PB20 stereo speakers offer a sound that is full of presence and depth. Designed for enhanced the bass of the PB20 bookshelf speaker system, calibrated with a flared bass reflex port. Resulting in rich detailed sound that will take your breath away. This flared bass reflex port is enhanced in a 100% medium-density fiberboard wooden enclosure. The wooden accent encompassing the full package of the speakers compliment both a home and office setting.

 

Mica PB20 Compact Bookshelf Speakers

Reliable Performance

 

The PB20 powered bookshelf speakers has combined a retro look with a modern sense of audio quality. If you’re looking for affordable and versatile bookshelf speakers with uncompromising sound the PB20 bookshelf speakers is your go-to 2.0 speaker system. Featuring a 3-inches bass driver and a calibrated, flared bass reflex port, this bookshelf speaker system delivers robust, rich bass notes that are full of presence and depth.

One Finger Control
